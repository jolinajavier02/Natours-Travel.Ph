Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour?

You can book through our website, call us, or visit our office. We'll guide you through the entire process and help you choose the perfect package.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, bank transfers, GCash, and PayMaya. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, cancellation and modification policies vary by service provider. Contact us as soon as possible for assistance.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we highly recommend travel insurance and can help you find the best coverage for your trip.

Are group discounts available?

Absolutely! We offer special rates for groups of 10 or more. Contact us for a customized quote.

Do you provide visa assistance?

Yes, we offer visa assistance services to help you with the application process for your destination.
