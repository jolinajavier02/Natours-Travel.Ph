Cookie Policy

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience, improve performance, and provide relevant content.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to help websites remember your preferences and activity.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies – Enable basic website functions
  • Performance Cookies – Collect anonymous usage data (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Functionality Cookies – Remember preferences such as language or region
  • Marketing Cookies – Show relevant travel ads or promotions

3. Why We Use Cookies

Cookies help us:

  • Improve site navigation
  • Enhance loading speed
  • Analyze visitor behavior to improve services
  • Personalize content and recommendations
  • Provide smoother booking experiences

4. Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. However, doing so may affect the functionality of some website features.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may come from trusted partners (e.g., analytics services). These help us monitor performance and provide better user experience.

By continuing to browse our site, you consent to the use of cookies as described above.
