Visa & Immigration Guides

Visa Assistance Overview

What We Do

  • Provide accurate visa requirements
  • Help fill out forms correctly
  • Book your embassy appointment
  • Review your documents
  • Provide sample cover letters
  • Assist with itinerary & travel proof
  • Give tips to avoid rejection

Why Applicants Get Denied

  • Weak financial proof
  • No strong country ties
  • Short notice travel
  • Fake documents
  • Missing documents
  • Applying for the wrong visa type

What Clients Receive After Payment

  • Complete visa requirements checklist
  • Written instructions per document
  • Appointment confirmation
  • Dedicated support
  • Sample templates (cover letter, COE, bank statement explanation letter, etc.)

Common Visa Mistakes

  • Passport less than 6 months valid
  • No travel history & weak itinerary
  • Fake bookings (very risky)
  • Incomplete requirements
  • Not showing strong ties (job, property, family)
  • Submitting unclear bank statements
  • Not writing a travel letter of intent properly

Immigration Red Flags

  • One-way ticket with no reason
  • No hotel booking
  • No travel insurance
  • No job or school proof
  • Nervous or inconsistent answers
  • Too much luggage for a short trip

Trip Planning & Money

Travel Insurance Guide

Why you need it: Covers medical emergencies, lost baggage, cancellations. Required for Schengen.

Types: Medical-only, Comprehensive, Student, Annual multi-trip.

Best Time to Travel

  • Peak (Expensive): Dec holidays, Summer (Jun-Aug), Major events
  • Off-Peak (Cheaper): Feb-Mar, Sep-mid Nov
  • Shoulder (Balanced): Apr-May, Late Oct

Money Tips

  • Check interbank rates
  • Exchange cash in home country
  • Withdraw abroad using debit cards
  • Avoid airport exchanges
  • Carry USD for emergencies
  • Use apps like XE Currency

Travel Itinerary Tips

Include: Day-by-day activities, Hotel address/contact, Flight details, Daily budget, Emergency contacts, Embassy details.

Travel Rights

When: Flight delayed, Luggage lost, Airline cancels, Denied boarding.

You may be eligible for: Hotel, Meals, Rebooking, Refund, Compensation.

Logistics & Tools

Airport Guide

  • Before: Check docs, print boarding pass, arrive 3hrs early, 100ml liquid limit.
  • During: Check-in, declare large money, avoid unnecessary chats.
  • Immigration: Answer purpose, duration, job, funds, return ticket briefly.

Baggage Guide

  • Carry-On: 7-10kg, no sharps, liquids <100ml.
  • Check-In: 20-30kg, wrap fragile items.
