Our Visa Assistance Service

Our Visa Assistance Service provides travelers with a smooth, guided, and stress-free experience when preparing visa applications. We assist you online by helping you understand the required documents, preparing all the needed forms, and booking your official visa appointment at VFS, embassies, or consulates.

Important: You remain the one who will visit the visa center personallyâ€”we provide full support, guidance, and preparation from start to finish.

What's Included in Our Visa Assistance Service

  • Assessment of Your Travel Purpose - We help identify the correct visa type (tourist, visit, transit, etc.) based on your travel plan and destination requirements.
  • Complete Visa Requirements Checklist - We guide you with a clear list of documents you must prepare, including:
    • Passport validity check
    • IDs, photos, COE, bank statements, ITR (if required)
    • Itinerary, accommodation proof, and flight reservation
    • Travel history and supporting documents
  • Filling Out Visa Application Forms - We assist in completing all necessary forms accurately and correctly.
  • Appointment Booking - We will book your official appointment at:
    • VFS Global
    • Embassies
    • Consulates
    • Other authorized visa centers
    You will only need to appear on your scheduled date.
  • Document Review Before Submission - We review your documents to ensure they follow the guidelines, match the embassy requirements, and are ready for submission.
  • Guidance on Fees & Payment Instructions - We provide information about visa fees, biometrics fees, and other embassy charges. (Note: Visa fees are paid directly to VFS/Embassyâ€”not to Natours Travel.)
  • Travel Advisory & Pre-Submission Reminders - We send reminders such as:
    • What to bring on the appointment day
    • Biometrics reminders
    • Embassy instructions
    • Estimated release time of results
  • Support for Re-application or Missing Requirements - If needed, we advise on how to update or correct documents.

Important Notes

To protect customers and maintain transparency, the following guidelines apply:

1. Natours Travel provides online assistance only.

  • We do not submit documents on your behalf.
  • Customers/applicants must personally attend their scheduled appointment to submit documents and undergo biometrics.

2. Visa Approval is NOT guaranteed.

  • Approval depends solely on the issuing embassy or consulate.
  • We assist with preparation but do not influence the outcome.

3. Visa-Required Destinations

  • For tours requiring a visa, you must secure your visa first before we can finalize your tour package booking.
  • This ensures smooth processing and avoids issues with immigration or booking changes.

4. Visa-Free Destinations

  • If the destination is visa-free for Philippine passport holders, you may book tours anytime without needing visa assistance.
